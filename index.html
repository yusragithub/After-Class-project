Nutritions

A well-balanced diet provides essential nutrients that fuel our bodies and support overall health. Nutrients such as carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, and minerals can be obtained from various sources including whole foods like fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean meats, and healthy fats. Additionally, fortified foods and supplements can also provide necessary nutrients. Taking adequate nutrition has numerous benefits, including maintaining healthy weight, supporting growth and development, boosting energy levels, enhancing mental clarity and focus, and reducing the risk of chronic diseases like heart disease, diabetes, and certain cancers. By prioritizing nutrition, individuals can optimize their overall well-being and quality of life. Here are some foods and their nutritions:-

Fruits

Apples: Fiber, Vitamin C, Antioxidants
Bananas: Potassium, Vitamin C, Vitamin B6
Oranges: Vitamin C, Vitamin A, Folate
Mangoes: Vitamin A, Vitamin C, Fiber

Vegetables

Broccoli: Vitamin C, Vitamin K, Fiber, Cancer-fighting compounds
Spinach: Iron, Vitamin A, Vitamin K, Folate
Carrots: Vitamin A, Fiber, Antioxidants
Tomatoes: Vitamin C, Lycopene (antioxidant)

Protein Sources

Chicken: Protein, Vitamin B6, Niacin
Salmon: Protein, Omega-3 fatty acids, Vitamin D
Eggs: Protein, Vitamin D, Choline
Lentils: Protein, Fiber, Iron, Zinc
